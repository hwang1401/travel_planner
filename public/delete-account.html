Account Deletion / 계정 삭제 안내

Last updated: March 2, 2026

How to Delete Your Account

You can request account deletion through the following methods:

  1. In-App: Open the app → Settings (설정) → Delete Account (계정 삭제)
  2. Email: Send a deletion request to travelunu@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include your registered email address.

Account deletion requests via email will be processed within 7 business days.

Data Deleted Upon Account Deletion

When your account is deleted, the following data will be permanently removed:

Data Type Action Retention Period
Profile information (email, name, profile photo) Deleted Deleted within 30 days
Travel itineraries you created Deleted Deleted within 30 days
Shared trip memberships Removed Immediate
Service usage logs Anonymized Anonymized within 30 days

Important Notes

  • Account deletion is irreversible. All data will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered.
  • If you are the owner of a shared trip, the trip will be deleted and collaborators will lose access.
  • Data required by law (e.g., transaction records) may be retained for the legally mandated period before deletion.

계정 삭제 방법

  1. 앱 내: 앱 실행 → 설정 → 계정 삭제
  2. 이메일: travelunu@gmail.com으로 "계정 삭제 요청" 제목으로 가입 이메일을 포함하여 요청

이메일 요청 시 영업일 기준 7일 이내에 처리됩니다.

삭제되는 데이터

  • 프로필 정보 (이메일, 이름, 프로필 사진) — 30일 이내 삭제
  • 생성한 여행 일정 — 30일 이내 삭제
  • 공유 여행 멤버십 — 즉시 제거
  • 서비스 이용 기록 — 30일 이내 익명화
